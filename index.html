Rocket Fiber

a brief summary

Rocket Fiber is a fiber optic internet, TV and business service company. They offer fiber optic services to the Detroit area.

The Challenges

Rocket Fiber is a new company, so they has no web presence. The goal was to increase awareness, as well as get people who would be interested in these services signed up to receive notices and updates when a new area is going live.

The Solution

Rocket Fiber got a fully fluid design, integrated into Wordpress. A contact form and newsletter subscription was added to the site. Transitions helped convey the modern look and feel of the website.

Life Chest

a brief summary

The Life Chest provides a special place to keep your most treasured memories. It replaces your old shoebox of family collectibles with an heirloom quality chest for all your keepsakes.

The Challenges

The Life Chest needed a home for their products, a place where customers could go online, browse through their items and place an order. They also needed a place where wholesalers could purchase their items in one easy click. On top of this, Life Chest wanted to keep everyone up to date with their latest happenings, via a blog and a social media stream.

The Solution

We decided it was best to integrate them into two Content Management Solutions, Wordpress, and Magento. With Wordpress hauling the load for the blog and content pages, and Magento doing the heavy lifting of the shopping cart component, we got down to business.

TheLifeChest.com got a new facelift, a fully responsive website integrated into Wordpress and Magento. I handled the front-end and back-end development, utilizing subtle transitions, float labels on their contact forms, and a custom section where their wholesalers could buy items at their wholesale price, and keep track of everything they've purchased.

AM Ginseng

a brief summary

AM Ginseng is a ginseng retailer for both retail and wholesale, as well as consumers. The offer 100% pure ginseng grown and produced in North America. They are a family owned and operated business that use stat of the art facilities to produce the best ginseng.

The Challenges

AM Ginseng wanted to stand out among the other ginseng retailers. They needed a strong web presence that caught the buyers' eyes. They offer packaged ginseng that goes through a rigorous routine to ensure the best quality. AM Ginseng wanted to tell their story, their process and not to only be known for their end product.

The Solution

AM Ginseng received a super modern and futuristic design. Each detail section is slanted, and the electric green really makes you want to stay on the page and read more. A nav section that smooth scrolls to the section to reveal more information sits atop the various sections to get customers the information they're looking for.

A full page navigation with a big lighting bolt slides in from the top when the navigation button is clicked. The individual sections, as well as the content pages slide in and become visible as the user scrolls to the section. A diagonal background fills the anchor links on hover to round off the subtle techniques used to really pull the entire site together.
